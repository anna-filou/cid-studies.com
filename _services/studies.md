    ## Technical Services

    - Facility layout studies
    - Facility management and assessment
    - Mapping of existing facilities
    - Process equipment and reactor design
      - Including settling tanks, bag filters, gas scrubbers, dryers, biofilters, beds, cyclones, hydrocyclones, heat exchangers, membrane bioreactors.
    - Preparation and analysis of user requirements, adaptation, parameterization and supervision of hardware/software systems in process facilities.
    - Materials
      - Development, design and quality control of materials.
      - Material characterization and standardization.
      - Selection and application of materials in facility equipment.
    - Chemical Studies and Research.
    - Chemical and Chemical Engineering Studies for projects, facilities and products.
    - Physicochemical and microbiological analyses and Control laboratory management.

    ## Water Treatment & Management

    - Complete solutions for water recovery and reuse from wastewater treatment plants
    - Disinfection – Deodorization of wastewater in pumping stations and treatment plants
    - Disinfection – Deodorization of industrial facilities, buildings etc.
    - Study and application of enzymatic products in biological treatment plants
    - Study, construction and installation of hypochlorous acid production equipment
      for safe, non-toxic disinfection of: - drinking water, - hydroponic nutrient solution, - industrial water (cooling towers, closed cooling circuits), - food and pharmaceutical industries
